## 🧠 What Capo Actually Is (Beyond a Music Player)

Capo is **not** a music streaming app, audio editor, or recording software.

Instead, it acts like a **digital music coach** that helps musicians break down songs into understandable pieces.

Traditionally, learning a song by ear involves:

* repeatedly rewinding sections
* slowing down recordings manually
* identifying chords one by one
* guessing notes through trial and error

Capo automates much of this process by analyzing audio and presenting musicians with visual tools that simplify learning.

Its goal is to help users spend **more time practicing and less time decoding songs**.
